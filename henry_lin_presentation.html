Timeline for Machine Learning Engineer

  • 01. Before school

      - Be familiar with Data Structure and Algorithm
      - Think of a side project to work on
      - Get your hands dirty on Big Data
      - Play Deep Learning with GPU if possible

  • 02. During the school

      - Study hard and find Professor to get involved in ML research projects
      - Work hard on system design, ML Algo, and spark stack so you can put it on resume.
      - Apply for volunteer/intern with ML startups on Angel list or Full-tim/Internship on Linkedin/Indeed/
      Monster/Company Websites

  • 03. 6 months before graduation

      - Ask your professor for recommendation letter
      - Coffee chat with professionals/alumni
      - Refine your resume and Apply as much as you can

  • 04. After Graduation

    • Get a Job if you are lucky
    • Keep Studying
    • Keep Hardworking
    • Keep Interviewing

Timeline for Data Analyst

  • 01. Before school

      - Be familiar with Python, R, and SQL
      - Think of a business case to work on
      - Get your hands dirty on Tableau
      - Play Shiny in R if possible

  • 02. During the school

      - Study hard and Practice your storytelling skill
      - Work hard on Data Visualization, Data Mining, and Applied ML so you can put it on resume.
      - Apply for volunteer/intern with Business startups on Angel list or Full-tim/Internship on Linkedin/Indeed/
      Monster/Company Websites

  • 03. 6 months before graduation

      - As many case studies as possible to shine your expertise
      - Coffee chat with professionals/alumni
      - Refine your resume and Apply as much as you can

  • 04. After Graduation

    • Get a Job if you are lucky
    • Keep Studying
    • Keep Hardworking
    • Keep Interviewing
